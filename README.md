# CareQuest MVP

AI-native workforce training for neurodivergence care.

---

## One-Line Thesis

We help ABAT/RBT training providers get more learners to finish and pass through AI-supported gamified certification training.

---

## Problem

The neurodivergence care market does not only have a therapy delivery problem.

It has a workforce creation problem.

Training today is:
- passive
- fragmented
- low engagement
- difficult to scale

Learners drop off before becoming job-ready.

---

## First Wedge

Training organizations running:
- ABAT
- RBT
- autism support certifications

---

## First User

Aspiring:
- ABAT learners
- RBT learners
- support professionals
- psychology students

---

## First Outcome

Increase certification completion rate.

---

## Product Concept

CareQuest turns certification training into a progression system.

1. Learner enters with a goal
2. AI creates adaptive pathway
3. Missions unlock progressively
4. Learner earns XP and badges
5. AI simulations test competency
6. Readiness score improves
7. Training providers track completion

---

## MVP Features

- AI onboarding assessment
- mission-based learning
- XP system
- streaks
- AI behavioral simulations
- mock readiness scoring
- cohort leaderboard

---

## Long-Term Vision

A workforce operating system for neurodivergence care.
