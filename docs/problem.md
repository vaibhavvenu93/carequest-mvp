# Problem

The biggest bottleneck in neurodivergence care is not demand.

It is trained human supply.

There are not enough:
- behavioral technicians
- shadow teachers
- autism support professionals
- special educators

Most training systems today are:
- passive
- fragmented
- difficult to scale
- low engagement

Learners drop off before becoming job-ready.

CareQuest explores whether:
- AI simulations
- progression systems
- gamified learning
- adaptive pathways

can improve learner completion and readiness.
